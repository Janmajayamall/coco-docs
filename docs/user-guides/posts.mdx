#### Creata a new Post

To post on a group's feed, click "New Post" on the left side panel of home page.

<Image
	src={require("./../../static/img/user-guides/new-post-button.jpg").default}
	caption={`Click on "New Post"`}
/>

Upload your post image by clicking on "Choose Image" & select the group to post to from "Select Group" dropdown. You will notice that you also have to provide values for "Liquidity" & "Bet for YES".

-   Liquidity - Amount in WETH for which you are supplying liquidity to your post's prediction market. It incentivises other users to place their bets on your post's predicition market.
-   Bet for YES - Amount in WETH for which your placing YES bet that your post is suitable for the group feed. Higher YES bet amount represents your high confidence in your new post.

After providing necessary values, click on "Post" to post. Since new post requires creating the post's prediction market on-chain, you will be asked to confirm transaction for creating the market. Once the transaction is processed, you have posted your post to group feed successfully.

<Image
	src={
		require("./../../static/img/user-guides/new-post-create-tx.jpg").default
	}
	caption={`Fill up details, click on "Post", and approve the transaction to Post`}
/>

#### Placing bets on post (Trading period)

You can select any post on your feed to place your bets. The **Live** text on post top right indicates that the post prediction market is in **Trading period**, thus open to bets.

<Image
	src={require("./../../static/img/user-guides/post-live-text.jpg").default}
	caption={`"Live" green text means post is in Trading Period. You can place your bets.`}
/>

Once you select a post that is Live (i.e. open to bets), you will find dashboard to place your bets on your right.

By default the dashboard is on "Buy". To place a YES bet, you buy YES shares and to place NO bet, you buy NO shares. Select either YES or NO and then enter amount in WETH for which you want buy shares.

Once you enter WETH amont, you will notice few values change.

-   Your choice - The type of share you are buying.
-   Estimated shares bought - Estimation of total shares that you will receive aftre buying.
-   Avg. price per share - Price in WETH that you pay for every share you receive.
-   Max. potential profit - Maximum profit in WETH that you will make, if your chosen outcome is declared as final outcome.

You will also notice that by default slippage is set to 0.5%. Setting slippage is required to prevent your order from executing at a unfavourable price caused by other orders.

Once you have selected your choice, entered WETH amount, and verified other values, click "Buy" to buy shares & confirm your order transaction.

<Image
	src={require("./../../static/img/user-guides/post-buy-yes-tx.jpg").default}
	caption={`After providing values, click "Buy" and approve transaction to place your bet.`}
/>

Once your transaction is processed, you should see your recently bought shares next to "You own" under the outcome of which shares you bought.

<Image
	src={
		require("./../../static/img/user-guides/post-you-own-shares.jpg")
			.default
	}
	caption={`Value next to "You own" represent shares you own of the outcome.`}
/>

If you don't see your shares, then wait for few seconds and reload page until it appears.

#### Challenge temporary outcomes (Challenge period)

If any post has "Challenge" in red on their top right, that post is in Challenge round & their Trading period has expired.

Select the post to challenge. On the right side, you will notice the challenge dashboard under "Challenge outcome". It displays the following values:

-   Temporary outcome - The current temporary outcome. If the outcome isn't challenged successfully before challenge period expires, it is declared the final outcome.
-   Time left to challenge - Time left for current challenge period to expire.
-   Challenges left - No. of challenges left before max. challenge limit is reached.
-   Min. amount to challenge - Amount in WETH to stake to challenge temporary outcome.
-   Outcome favored - The outcome that you think is the right outcome and in favor of which you challenge the temporary outcome.

To challenge enter the amount greater than equal to Min. amount to challenge and click Challenge button. Approve the transaction and wait for it process.

<Image
	src={
		require("./../../static/img/user-guides/post-challenge-outcome-tx.jpg")
			.default
	}
	caption={`Enter min. challenge amount & click on Challenge to challenge temporary outcome.`}
/>

Once your transaction is processed, your challenge is successful. You must see your favored outcome as the new temporary outcome & challenge period renewed.

#### Resolution Period

If max. no. of challenges are reached for any post's prediction market, the market enters the Resolution period. That is, the market is waiting for governing body of the group to declare the final outcome.

A post in resolution period will have "Resolution" text on its top right corner.

#### Redeem Wins

Once the final outcome is declared, either as the temporary outcome or governing body declared it, the market ends. Post's with their prediction market ended, will have "Ended" text on their top right. In "Ended" state you can redeem your wins.

Select the post from which you want to redeem your wins. On the right side you will notice, the "final outcome", a "Claim" button, along with some values.

-   Your trade winnings - Your initial investment + profit by buying shares of the right outcome (i.e. final outcome).
-   In favour of YES - Amount that you staked for challenge in favour of YES outcome
-   In favour of NO - Amount that you staked for challenge in favour of NO outcome
-   Challenge winnings - Your reward for staking for the right outcome
-   You receive from Challenge - Amount you staked for the righr outcome + challenge winnings
-   You get back in total - Amount in Your trade winnings + You receive from Challenge.
